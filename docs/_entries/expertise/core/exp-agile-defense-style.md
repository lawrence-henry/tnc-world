#### :material-shield: Agile Defense Styles {#exp-agile-defense-style}

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
!!! abstract ""
    **Type:** Style

    **Tags:** Defense, Melee, Reaction, Mobility

    **Prerequisites:** Trained in Martial Arts, No Equipped Armor

    Defense is not optional for you. Your art is your bond. Your spirit is your guide. You seek safety in the heart of danger.

    === "Overview"
        **Role:** Reactive defense and counter-pressure
        **Playstyle:** Stay mobile, bait attacks, punish mistakes

        |Benefits||
        |:------:|:-|
        |**Basic:**|+2 Defense, Speed Boost|
        |**Initiate:**| Improved Defense Bonuses, Improvised Cover|
        |**Advanced:**| Swift-Shift (1 CP), Brutal Counter (2 CP)|
        |**Expert:**| Passive Defense +5, Unique Feature - **Only The Strong**|

    === "Basic"
        You gain **+2 Defense**.

        Once per round, when you are targeted by an attack, your Speed increases by **1 + ½ level** (round down) yards until the start of your next turn.

    === "Initiate"
        Using the **Defend** Minor Action while in melee combat grants you a bonus to your Defense equal to half of the Defense Bonus of one chosen opponent within your Melee Range until the start of your next turn.

        A target chosen this way becomes Improvised Cover against Ranged Attack Rolls and Area Effect attacks.

        Additionally, when you use the Defend Action, you gain a Major Advantage on your next attack roll against a target within your Melee Range. If this benefit is not used before the end of your next turn, it is lost.

    === "Advanced"
        - **Swift-Shift (1 CP):** Use **Defend** as an Instinct Action.
        - **Brutal Counter (2 CP):** If an enemy misses their attack within your Melee Range due to your use of the Defend Action, you may add half of your Reflexes Ability and the target's last used Attack Bonus to your next Melee Damage Roll. If your attack already uses Reflexes, use your Willpower Ability for the bonus instead.

    === "Expert"
        - Defense bonus increases to **+5**.
        - **Only The Strong:** If an enemy misses you with an attack within your Melee Range due to your use of the Defend Action, that enemy takes your Unarmed or Melee Attack Damage as part of their action.
