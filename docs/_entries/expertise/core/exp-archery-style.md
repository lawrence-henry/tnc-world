#### :material-bullseye: Archery Style {#exp-archery-style}

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
!!! abstract ""
    **Type:** Style
    **Tags:** Offense, Ranged
    **Prerequisites:** Trained with Archaic Ranged Weapons; Archaic Ranged Weapon Equipped

    Your arm is steady, and your aim is true. Although your weapon has been overshadowed by conventional fighting methods, that doesn't mean it can be safely overlooked.

    === "Overview"
        **Role:** Active offense

        |Benefits||
        |:------:|:-|
        |**Basic:**|Ignore 4 AP; Increased Range.|
        |**Initiate:**|+2 Aim; Faster Reload. |
        |**Advanced:**| Faster Attack |
        |**Expert:**| Ignore Cover|

    === "Basic"
        Your Ranged Attacks using an Archaic Ranged Weapon gain the Armor-Piercing Property, ignoring up to 4 points of a target's AP.

        Additionally, increase all Weapon Range Increments with these weapons by 5 + 1/2 level (round down) yards.

    === "Initiate"
        - **Focused Shot:** When you use the Aim Minor Action, gain an additional +2 bonus on all Ranged Attack Rolls until the end of your turn.
        - **Rapid Reload:** You can reload a bowgun as a Minor Action. All other Archaic Ranged Weapons can be reloaded as a Free Action.
    === "Advanced"
        - **Rain of Arrows:** While using an Archaic Ranged Weapon, you can treat your Ranged Attack Roll with this kind of weapon as a Minor Action.

        - Additionally, you can perform the **Lightning Attack** for 2 CP instead of the usual 3.
    === "Expert"
        - **Masterful Aim:** While using an Archaic Ranged Weapon, you can ignore up to 3/4 cover.
