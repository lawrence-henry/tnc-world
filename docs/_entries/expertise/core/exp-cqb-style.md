#### :material-sword-cross: CQB Style {#exp-cqb-style}

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
!!! abstract ""
    **Type:** Style

    **Tags:** Offense, Melee, Ranged
    
    **Prerequisites:** Trained in Martial Arts; Ranged Weapon Equipped

    Close quarters excite you. You've made being too close to an enemy into an artform.

    === "Overview"
        **Role:** Balanced Offense/Defense

        |Benefits||
        |:------:|:-|
        |**Basic:**|Activate + Aim on Initiative, Hard to Disarm|
        |**Initiate:**| Riposte 2 CP, Ranged Counterattack |
        |**Advanced:**| Double-Tap (2 CP) |
        |**Expert:**| Moving Target (4 CP)|

    === "Basic"
        You can use the Activate and Aim Minor Actions as part of your Initiative Roll. Additionally, any attempt to Disarm you is made at a Major Disadvantage (-5).
    === "Initiate"
        As long as you are within CQB range of an opponent, you can use the Riposte Chance Action as an Instinct Action for 2 CP instead of the usual 4. Additionally, you can use a Ranged Attack for the Riposte counterattack instead of a Melee Attack. This allows you to use Riposte against Ranged Attacks that miss you.
    === "Advanced"
        - **Double-Tap (2 CP):** You can make a single Ranged Attack Roll count as making 2 Ranged Attacks using the same Major Action, dealing damage with both as normal. These attacks must be aimed at the same target, and you must have at least two pieces of ammunition loaded into your weapon.
    === "Expert"
        - **Moving Target (4 CP):** When you are targeted by a Ranged Attack while in CQB range with another enemy, you can use your Instinct Action to impose a Major Disadvantage (-5) on the attack roll. If the attack misses, you can immediately move up to 2 yards without provoking attacks of opportunity. If the attack misses by 5 or less, it hits the enemy you were in CQB range with instead of you.
