#### :material-crowd: Lone Wolf Style {#exp-lone-wolf-style}

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
!!! abstract ""
    **Type:** Style

    **Tags:** Defense
    
    **Prerequisites:** Perception 3 or Higher, Reflexes 3 or Higher, Trained in Martial Arts

    Your opponents' final thoughts are generally about how foolish it was to surround you.

    === "Overview"
        **Role:** Defense

        |Benefits||
        |:------:|:-|
        |**Basic:**| Can't be outnumbered. |
        |**Initiate:**| Bonus Defense, Dual Strike (2 CP)|
        |**Advanced:**| Dance of the Reaper (1+ CP)|
        |**Expert:**| Broken Pack|

    === "Basic"
        As long as you remain outnumbered, you ignore Advantage gained on attacks targeting you due to number of enemies.
    === "Initiate"
        As long as you remain outnumbered, your Defense gains a bonus equal to the number of targets whose Advantage has been ignored. Additionally, you can use the Dual Strike Chance Action for 2 CP instead of 4.
    === "Advanced"
        - **Dance of the Reaper(1+ CP):** Your movement speed increases by 5 yards per CP spent. Make a series of Melee or Ranged attacks against a number of targets equal to CP spent, as long as you can reasonably reach them with your movement. This is made as a single Attack Action Roll, compared to each target's Defense, dealing Chance Damage on each, unless you roll triples, in which case you deal Critical Damage.
    === "Expert"
        - **Broken Pack:** As long as you remain outnumbered, you gain Major Advantage on a number of your Attacks equal to the number of targets whose Advantage was ignored.
