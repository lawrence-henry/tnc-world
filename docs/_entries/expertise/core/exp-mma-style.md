#### :material-karate: MMA Style {#exp-mma-style}

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
!!! abstract ""
    **Type:** Style

    **Tags:** Adaptive
    
    **Prerequisites:** Trained in Martial Arts

    You have trained in a variety of Martial Arts Styles, adapting what you like and discarding the rest. No opponent knows quite what to expect from you next.

    **Note:** Benefits from this style do not stack with their original style's benefits (for example, you can only use Basic Striking Style's benefits using MMA Style or Striking Style, but not both at the same time).

    === "Overview"
        **Role:** Adaptable, Offense, Defense

        |Benefits||
        |:------:|:-|
        |**Basic:**| Any Basic Style, switch 5 times per Rest. |
        |**Initiate:**| Any Initiate Style, switch 2 times per Rest. |
        |**Advanced:**| Any Advanced Style, switch 1 time per Rest. |
        |**Expert:**| Any Expert Style, Fixed. |

    === "Basic"
        Pick one Basic Tier Ability of any Expertise with the Style Tag. 5 times per Rest, as a Free Action, you can swap this ability for another Basic Tier Ability. You can only gain the benefits from one Basic Tier Ability in this way at a time.
    === "Initiate"
        Pick one Initiate Tier Ability of any Expertise with the Style tag. 2 times per Rest, as a Minor Action, you can swap this ability for another Initiate Tier Ability. You can only gain the benefits from one Initiate Tier Ability in this way at a time.
    === "Advanced"
        - Pick one Advanced Tier Ability of any Expertise with the Style tag. 1 time per rest, as a Major Action, you can swap this ability for another Advanced Tier Ability. You can only gain the benefits from one Advanced Tier Ability in this way at a time.
    === "Expert"
        - Pick one Expert Tier Ability of any Expertise with the Style Label. This ability can only be swapped during an extended Rest (i.e.: as a downtime activity). You can only gain the benefits from one Expert Tier Ability in this way at a time.
