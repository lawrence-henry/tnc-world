#### :material-pistol: Small-Arms Expert Style {#exp-small-arms-expert}

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
!!! abstract ""
    **Type:** 

    **Tags:** 
    
    **Prerequisites:** Trained in Small-Arms

    Your skill with small firearms is something to behold.

    === "Overview"
        **Role:** 

        |Benefits||
        |:------:|:-|
        |**Basic:**| Draw and Holster as a Non-Action. Reload as an Instinct Action. |
        |**Initiate:**| Aim gives +2 bonus. |
        |**Advanced:**| Deadeye Shot (2 CP) |
        |**Expert:**| Lethal Focus |

    === "Basic"
    You can draw and holster a Small-Arms weapon as a Non-Action. Additionally, you can reload a Small-Arms Weapon as an Instinct Action.        
    === "Initiate"
    When you use Aim while wielding a Small-Arms Weapon, you gain a +2 bonus to your Attack Roll instead of the usual +1.   
    === "Advanced"
        - **Deadeye Shot (2 CP):** Once per Round, you can reroll a single failed Attack Roll with a Small-Arms weapon, and may use either result.
    === "Expert"
        - **Lethal Focus:** While using a Small-Arms Weapon, if you roll Doubles, your Damage ignores up to 4 AP provided by a target's armor or cover. If you roll triples, your Damage ignores up to 6 AP provided by a target's armor or cover.
