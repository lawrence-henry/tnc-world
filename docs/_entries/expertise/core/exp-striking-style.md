#### :material-mixed-martial-arts: Striking Style {#exp-striking-style}

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
!!! abstract ""
    **Type:** 

    **Tags:** 
    
    **Prerequisites:** 

    === "Overview"
        **Role:** 

        |Benefits||
        |:------:|:-|
        |**Basic:**| 2 Attacks per Round |
        |**Initiate:**| 3 Attacks per Round, Trap and Box (1 CP) |
        |**Advanced:**| Precision Strike (2 CP) |
        |**Expert:**| 1 Instinct Action Attack per Round, or 4 Attacks per Round |

    === "Basic"
        Make an Unarmed Attack as a Minor Action, allowing you up to 2 Attacks per Round.
    === "Initiate"
        Make an Unarmed Attack as a Free Action, allowing you up to 3 Attacks per Round.
        
        - **Trap and Box (1 CP):** When attacked, instead of relying on your regular Defense, you can roll an opposed Unarmed Attack. On a success, you stop the target's attack and initiate a Grapple, and your next Unarmed Attack gains +2 Damage. On a failure, the target's Attack deals an additional +2 damage.
    === "Advanced"
        - **Precision Strike (2 CP):** When using a Chance Action that increases damage in combination with your Unarmed Attack, add an additional +4 to that Damage.
    === "Expert"
        Make an Unarmed Attack as an Instinct Action, allowing up to 4 Attacks per Round. This would alternatively allow 1 Attack as an Instinct Action on another character's turn, and up to 3 regular Attacks. This does not grant additional Instinct Actions.
        
        - **Ultra Combination:** While using the Lightning Attack Chance Action, when you roll Doubles, gain additional, cumulative Chance Points equal to your Might or Reflexes (whichever is higher). These points must be used for additional Lightning Attacks immediately, or be lost. You can gain these points a number of times equal to your Might or Reflexes (whichever is higher) in additional Chance Actions per Round through this ability.
