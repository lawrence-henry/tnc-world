#### :material-arm-flex-outline: Wrestling Style {#exp-wrestling-style}

<!-- prettier-ignore -->
!!! abstract ""
    **Type:** 

    **Tags:** 
    
    **Prerequisites:** Trained in Strength

    Throws and takedowns are child's play for you. Anyone getting close enough to grab is going to have a bad day if you have anything to say about it.

    === "Overview"
        **Role:** 

        |Benefits||
        |:------:|:-|
        |**Basic:**| +2 bonus to Grapples |
        |**Initiate:**| Free Action Grapple after Unarmed Hit |
        |**Advanced:**| Slip Grab (2+ CP), Suplex (1+ CP) |
        |**Expert:**| Iron Grip (4 CP), Throw Combination (6+ CP), Hurricane Throw (6 CP) |

    === "Basic"
        Gain a +2 bonus to all Action Rolls related to Grapples.
    === "Initiate"
        When you successfully hit a target with an Unarmed Attack, you can make an immediate Grapple attempt as a Free Action.
    === "Advanced"
        - **Slip Grab (2+ CP):** If you are missed by a Melee or Unarmed Attack, you can perform a special Grapple and Throw maneuver as an Instinct Action, dealing your normal Grapple Strike Damage. Your target lands Prone a number of yards away equal to your Chance Die. You can keep hold of the Grapple and refuse the throw distance by spending an additional +4 CP.
        - **Suplex (1+ CP):** You can perform the Knock Prone Chance Action for 1 CP instead of the usual 2. You can keep hold of the Grapple by spending an additional +1 CP.
    === "Expert"
        - **Iron Grip (4 CP):** While you are in control of a Grapple, you can spend your Instinct Action to impose a Major Disadvantage (-5) to all of your held target's Might or Reflexes-related Action Rolls until they break free, or the start of your next turn, whichever comes first.
        - **Throw Combination (6+ CP):** You slam your target on the ground, or into an object, multiple times. This attack deals your Grapple Strike Damage once for every 3 CP spent into it. If slammed into an object, add an amount of Damage equal to the AP value of the object's material.
        - **Hurricane Throw (6 CP):** Make a Grapple Attempt. You jump onto your target's shoulders and flip backwards, throwing them in the process. This attack deals twice your Reflexes in Bludgeoning Damage. If the target strikes an object, this Chance Action deals an additional amount of Damage equal to the AP value of the object's material.
