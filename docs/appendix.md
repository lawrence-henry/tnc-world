## Items Properties Index

This is a list of features common to various items and weapons, both to define items listed in this book, and in the event a player or GM wishes to create custom equipment. For particularly specific items, GMs are encouraged to adjust prices appropriately.

### Armor

**Active Camouflage:**
: Specially woven to blend with the environment around it. Grants a Minor Advantage on Reflexes (Stealth) tests relying on remaining unseen.

**Covert:**
: Can be worn under other clothing items. CR 10 + your Constitution score is required to spot the armor.

**Cut Resistant:**
: Retains full AP against weapons that deal slashing or piercing damage.

**Extra Pockets:**
: Gain a bonus +2 Units of Carrying Capacity.

**Hazard Seals:**
: In the event of a puncture, armor with Hazard Seals automatically cinches off portions to keep a hermetic seal. Grants a Major Advantage on Saving Throws against airborne hazards.

### Weapons

**5-Cylinder:**
: Holds up to 5 bullets in the weapon at a time. Bullets are loaded into the cylinder at a rate of 1 Round per Minor Action. The cylinder revolves after every trigger pull, cycling the next loaded Round into the weapon's chamber.

**6-Cylinder:**
: Holds up to 6 bullets in the weapon at a time. Bullets are loaded into the cylinder at a rate of 1 Round per Minor Action. The cylinder revolves after every trigger pull, cycling the next loaded Round into the weapon's chamber.

**Acid Damage:**
: When used against objects, it moves the item one step lower on the Item Destruction Chart per round until that Item is Completely Destroyed. When used against a biological target, it decreases Armor Protection (AP) from all sources by 1 per round. Worn armor at 0 AP is useless weight, and will no longer protect you from damage. For characters with the Hardened Skin Special Ability Mutation, lost AP is restored by 1 AP per Rest. Every Round after the initial hit, a creature can attempt a Reflexes Saving Throw CR 15 to end the effect. Any creature with an AP of 0 while affected by Acid takes 10 Damage at the start of every turn until they pass the Saving Throw.

**AP-Tuned:**
: When fired at any non-living character or object, the loaded ammunition is treated as AP ammo, regardless of whether or not it's actually loaded, allowing the damage of the weapon to ignore an additional 6 AP provided by material.

**Armor-Piercing:**
: Ignores up to 4 AP provided by armor or cover.

**Auto-Fire:**
: The weapon is capable of firing in fully automatic bursts. When a weapon is used this way, it hits a 5-yard cone area, and uses 10 bullets. If the weapon has less than 10 bullets loaded, the size of the affected area decreases by 1 yard per 2 missing bullets.

**Ballistic Damage:**
: Damage caused by firearms.

**Blast Damage:**
: Damage caused by explosive weapons and devices. Unless otherwise specified, this damage hits a 4 yard radius area.

**Bleed:**
: On rolling doubles, before determining Chance Actions, the attacking character can spend 1 CP. The target makes a Constitution Saving Throw, CR 10 + the value on the Attacker's Chance Die, or gain 1 Tier of the Bleeding Condition.

**Bludgeoning Damage:**
: Damage caused by blunt items and weapons.

**Box Magazine:**
: A detachable magazine that can be inserted into a weapon as a Minor Action, providing Ammunition to be fired. Expended Ammunition Casings are usually ejected by the weapon in order to load the next bullet in the cycle.

**Burst-Fire:**
: The weapon is capable of firing in short bursts with every pull of the trigger, spending 5 bullets, and granting a Minor Advantage to the Attack Roll. The weapon must have at least 5 bullets loaded to benefit from this bonus. A Character with the Rifleman Style Expertise can choose to expend only 3 bullets to gain the benefit, but the weapon must have 3 bullets loaded into it.

**Cheap Ammo:**
: The weapon can use just about any object of the proper size as ammunition. This weapon's ammunition need not be tracked, as it's likely a character is surrounded by objects that can be used.

**Cold Damage:**
: On rolling doubles, before determining Chance Actions, the attacking character can spend 1 CP. The target makes a Might Saving Throw, CR 10 + the value on the Attacker's Chance Die, or gain 1 Tier of the Hypothermic Condition.

**Concealable:**
: While this weapon/item is on your person and remains undrawn, a Perception check CR 10 + your Reflexes Score is required to spot the weapon.

**CP Boost:**
: On rolling doubles, the weapon grants an additional bonus to Chance Points equal to half the value of the Chance Die.

**Double:**
: The weapon has two damaging ends, allowing for a second attack as a Minor Action.

**Energy Damage:**
: Damage caused by plasma-based weapons, which completely ignores AP provided by armor. Additionally, chance to cause Burning Condition. Must be GM-approved.

**Fast Reload:**
: Reloading this weapon is a Free Action, leaving your Minor Action for other uses.

**Finesse:**
: A weapon with this property can be used with either the Might or Reflexes Ability, user's choice.

**Fire Damage:**
: On rolling doubles, before determining Chance Actions, the attacking character can spend 1 CP. The target makes a Reflexes Saving Throw, CR 10 + the value on the Attacker's Chance Die, or gain 1 Tier of the Burning Condition.

**Flash:**
: On rolling doubles, before determining Chance Actions, the attacking character can spend 1 CP. The target makes a Constitution Saving Throw, CR 10 + the value on the Attacker's Chance Die, or gain 1 Tier of the Impaired Sense (Sight) Condition.

**Heavy:**
: Imposes a -2 penalty to Reflexes when determining Movement Speed.

**High Caliber:**
: On rolling triples, before determining Chance Actions, the attacker can spend 3 CP. The target makes a Constitution Saving Throw, CR 10+ the value on the Attacker's Chance Die, or the Damage dealt will not heal until the target has received Surgery.

**Improvised:**
: An item with this property has primary functions that have nothing to do with weaponry or causing damage.

**Internal Magazine:**
: The magazine is an integral part of the weapon, and cannot be removed. Rounds can be fed into it at a rate of 1 Round per Minor Action, up to its full Ammunition Capacity. Those Rounds are then cycled by the action of the weapon.

**Ionic Damage:**
: A character hit by an Ionic weapon must make a Constitution Saving Throw CR 10 + the Attacker's Chance Die, or gain a number of Tiers of the Stunned Condition equal to the Target's Chance Die (a result of 6 allows the Target to ignore the Condition). Electronic Equipment hit by this type of damage becomes inoperable for 5 Minutes (20 Rounds). If said item is hit by Ionic Damage a second time before the time it needs to reboot, it becomes inoperable until repaired.

**Knockdown:**
: This weapon delivers a significant amount of force to a target. On rolling doubles, before determining Chance Actions, the attacking character can spend 1 CP. The target makes a Constitution Saving Throw, CR 10 + the value on the Attacker's Chance Die, or fall Prone.

**Leverage:**
: Weapons and items with this property grant a Major Advantage (+5) when making a Might (Strength) Action Roll to pry open or break an object.

**Piercing Damage:**
: Damage caused by bows, and some pointed weapons.

**Precise:**
: Attack rolls made within a weapon's CQB, Short, or Medium Range Increments gain an additional +2 bonus to the Attack Roll.

**Reach:**
: Allows a weapon to be used in Melee Range at up to 4-yards away.

**Reliable:**
: The weapon never jams, and only misfires on a roll of 3 on 3d6, regardless of doubles.

**Safety Switch:**
: The weapon can be made not to fire, allowing for safer handling of the firearm. Using the switch is a Minor Action.

**Select-Fire:**
: The weapon has a switch that allows its firing mode to be changed. The firing modes available are detailed in the weapon's description. Switching between firing modes is a Minor Action, and your firing mode must be chosen before you make your attack roll.

**Semi-Auto:**
: Every pull of the trigger fires a single bullet from the chamber of the weapon.

**Silent:**
: The weapon makes no noise, and does not break Stealth when used.

**Single Shot:**
: The weapon must be primed each time it's fired, requiring a Minor Action to feed a round into the chamber.

**Slashing Damage:**
: Damage caused by weapons with a bladed edge.

**Sonic:**
: On rolling doubles, before determining Chance Actions, the attacking character can spend 1 CP. The target makes a Constitution Saving Throw, CR 10 + the value on the Attacker's Chance Die, or gain 1 Tier of the Impaired Sense (Hearing) Condition.

**Splash:**
: Effect or Damage is spread across an area. Unless otherwise specified, this spread is 4 yards.

**Spray:**
: If the weapon this property is applied to is used to make an Auto-Fire attack, the attack roll suffers a Minor Disadvantage (-2), but increases the damage cone size by 5 yards.

**Spread-Fire:**
: The weapon fires in a cone of damage. The size of the cone is dependent on the Range the weapon is fired from, the width of which is equal to the weapon's range increment. Each range increment reduces the damage by 2.

**Stun:**
: On rolling doubles, before determining Chance Actions, the attacking character can spend 1 CP. The target makes a Constitution Saving Throw, CR 10 + the value on the Attacker's Chance Die, or gain 1 Tier of the Stunned Condition.

**Thrown:**
: The weapon can be thrown with some precision. Weapons without this property that are thrown are treated as Improvised Ranged Weapons.

**Trigger Safety:**
: The Safety Switch is a part of the trigger mechanism, that prevents accidental firing while allowing the weapon to be fired without hesitation.

**Two-Handed:**
: The weapon cannot be properly used with fewer than two hands.

**Unreliable:**
: The weapon is prone to malfunctions. The weapon misfires on a 5 or 6 on 3d6, and jams on a 3 or 4 on 3d6.

**Versatile:**
: This weapon can be used with one or two hands. If used with two hands, add the Minimum Might Requirement as a bonus to the Weapon's Damage.

### Explosives

**Bleed:**
: Applies one or more Tiers of the Bleeding Condition on a failed save. The number of Tiers applied is specified in the weapon's description.

**Blind:**
: Applies T3 of the Impaired Sense (Sight) Condition on a failed save. This effect decreases by 1 Tier at the beginning of each of the target's subsequent turns.

**Color-Code:**
: Weapon can be purchased in a variety of colors to fit various tactical communication scenarios.

**Concealment:**
: Weapon creates an area of visual concealment. Attacking a completely concealed character adds +5 to that target's Defense.

**Destructive:**
: When damaging non-organic targets, ignores up to 20 AP before determining the results of damage.

**Directional:**
: Has a front that can be placed, so that the explosive can fire in a cone of damage ahead of it.

**Ion Blast:**
: Applies the effects of Ionic Damage as if it was Blast Damage. Unless otherwise specified, this damage hits a 4 yard radius area, ignoring any cover not shielded against EMP attacks, but does not affect organic targets.

**Shapeable:**
: Weapon can be molded into various shapes, allowing it to be directional if needed, focusing the weapon's damage into specific points. When used this way, the user removes a number of unintended targets from their Area of Effect equal to their Wits Ability.

**Spread:**
: The contents of this weapon spread at a rate of 1-yard per turn, causing its effects within the cloud area. This cloud can go around corners and through gaps at least an inch wide, but can be dispersed by heavy winds.

**Timed:**
: This weapon explodes following the next Action after being used. Multiple applications extend this time by 1 Action, up to 1 minute.

### Vehicles

**All-Terrain:**
: Ignores difficult terrain penalties for most natural environments (mud, sand, snow, etc.), but reduces Top Speed by 1 category.

**Autonomous:**
: The vehicle has an onboard AI capable of basic self-navigation, though it still requires a human operator for combat maneuers and other complex actions.

**Electric Vehicle (EV):**
: Requires at least 1 hour of charging per hour of use. Maximum of 6 hours on a full charge.

**Fuel Efficient:**
: Consumes fuel at half the rate of a standard model of its type, but reduces the Top Speed by 1 category.

**High Performance:**
: Consumes fuel at double the rate of a standard model of its type, but increases Top Speed by 1 category.

**Manual Transmission:**
: Requires additional time to shift gears, but allows better speed control. All maneuvers are Major Actions, but reduce Speed penalties to Maneuver by 3.

**Ruggedized:**
: Designed for durability, this vehicle reduces incoming damage from collisions by 1 category at the expense of Top Speed (A collision at Highway Speed deals Street Speed damage).

**Tow Hook:**
: Vehicle can carry up to an additional half of its standard Cargo capacity.
